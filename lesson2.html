Lesson 2: Using the Learning Platform

In this lesson, you'll learn how to navigate and use the Kael 5000 platform effectively. The platform is designed to be user-friendly and can be accessed using your phone, tablet, or computer.

What you'll learn:

  • How to log in and access your dashboard
  • Where to find lessons and activities
  • How to submit assignments or complete quizzes
  • How to contact support if needed

Start by exploring your dashboard. Each lesson is clearly labeled. Click on one to begin. Use the menu to move between sections and check your progress as you go.
